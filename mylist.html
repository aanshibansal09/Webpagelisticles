Sector-7 cafes that you must try

COFFEES AND CAFE

1.
HEDGEHOG CAFE
SCF 12, Inner Market, Sector 7-C, Sector 7,

Very chill atmosphere; I could work in a relaxed manner along with a nice sip of coffee. As a plus, the wifi connectivity is good too.This small restaurant has lounge feel for people to unwind, feel into books and pick up some good tasty stuff to satiate your palette. I liked the little blueberry cake baked over crisp and crunchy cooky base. With books lined up on walls, it's perfect place to have a great coffee break.

Recommendation:

blueberry cake baked over crisp and crunchy cooky base

2.
THIRD WAVE COFFEE
Ground Floor, SCO - 4, Madhya Marg, Sector 7-C, Sector 7,

Third Wave Coffee was born with the intention of introducing India to a perfectly brewed cup of coffee. Our vision is to curate the best experiences over the freshest coffee, both at our cafes and at home. Our selections are sourced from the top producers in the country and are handpicked, carefully roasted and used to make a variety of caffeine creations. We truly believe that coffee has the power to start conversations and build communities.

Recommendation:

Pistachio Frappe

3.
STARBUCKS
Madhya Marg, Sector 7-C, Sector 7, Chandigarh,

Seattle-based coffeehouse chain known for its signature roasts, light bites and WiFi availability.One of the most trusted brand with its products and services.

Recommendation:

Latte macchiato
